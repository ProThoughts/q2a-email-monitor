    Q2A Email Monitor

    Plugin for Q2A that monitors an email address and does actions based on the subject and content handed to it.

    This is a work in progress. The intention is to create a plugin that can respond to emails sent to an email address that the plugin monitors. By doing this, you can have an action be performed upon receipt of an email such as creating a new question or commenting on a question.
